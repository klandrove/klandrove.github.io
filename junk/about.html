About Me

Practical Creative Technologist

I was born and raised in South Florida, proudly embracing my Cuban heritage, including the language and traditions passed down by my grandparents. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Interactive Media from the University of Miami.

I am passionate about creating unique and accessible user experiences on the web, whether from scratch in an empty HTML file or using frameworks such as Bootstrap. I have knowledge of full-stack development, a variety of data analytic tools, quality assurance principles, and scrum methodologies.

As the Vice President of Marketing for Kappa Theta Pi Sigma Chapter, a professional technology fraternity, I frequently collaborate with diverse individuals who share a passion for technology. Outside of class, you can find me at sporting events, especially baseball and hockey games. Sometimes, you may even find me on the field as a ball girl!

I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges, striving to blend creativity with technical skills to build engaging digital experiences.
